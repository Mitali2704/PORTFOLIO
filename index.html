👩‍💻 About Me

Hello! I'm Mitali Brahmankar, a passionate Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering student from PCCOE, Pune. I have a strong interest in embedded systems, IoT, FPGA design, AI/ML, and control systems.

With hands-on experience in Arduino, MATLAB Simulink, Verilog, Python, and various microcontrollers, I enjoy building innovative projects that solve real-world problems. I have completed internships in VLSI design and worked on multiple technical projects ranging from smart IoT devices to AI-based wildlife detection systems.

I'm a winner at INNOVISION 2025 (Techathlon) organized by JSPM Group of Institutes, and have worked on the Smart India Hackathon (SIH) project for elephant detection. I constantly strive to learn new technologies and apply them to create impactful solutions.
